A short story about me , my mission, thinking and craft.

Former Firefighter, Business Owner and current Software Engineer.

I became a Software Engineer during the midst of Covid. During that time, I was working as a Home Inspector for the business that I owned. I truly enjoyed owning my own business, but the work it self left something to be desired.

So, I decided to make a change. The only things that I knew I wanted out of a career was for it to, stimulate my brain, give me the opportunity to make an impact, and have the ability to create something.

I ended up taking a few online classes to see what programming was all about and realized farely quickly that this something I could see myself doin for the rest of my life. From there, I decided it was time to get serious, so I signed up for a bootcamp and never looked back.

After a couple of years, I am currently an Instructor at the full stack software engineering bootcamp that I signed up for. On a day to day basis, I manage and guide a cohort through all the learning objectives of full stack development. This includes daily content/curriculum creation and live lectures over technologies such as SQL, Javascript, Python, React/Redux. Express, Sequelize, Cloud Technologies, AWS, WebSockets, Docker, Kubernetes, and more. During my tenure at this job, I have relied on my exemplary communication skills to bring over 100 students to graduation and manage/oversee over 60 projects to production. I am an extremely capable software engineer, who has been building their skill set for many years now and can’t wait to speak with you more about what I can bring to your team.
